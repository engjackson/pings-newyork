Our Menu

Experience the vibrant taste of Hong Kong at Ping's Restaurant. Our authentic dim sum is **handcrafted daily** by skilled chefs, and our **live seafood** is prepared straight from the tank to your table. We bring the freshest ingredients and traditional flavors to the heart of **New York City's historic Chinatown**, offering an unforgettable dining experience.
